I. Student Progress Reports:
1. Individual Student Progress Report:
   * Content: Current Juz/Surah/Ayah being memorized, total memorized (Juz/Surahs/Ayahs), number of new Ayahs/Surahs memorized per week/month, average memorization rate, last revision date, next revision due date, performance on recent tests/recitations, challenges identified.
   * Purpose: Provides a comprehensive overview of a single student's journey, helps identify areas needing focus.
2. Memorization Milestone Report:
   * Content: List of students who have completed a certain Juz, a specific Surah, or a certain percentage of the Quran.
   * Purpose: Celebrates achievements, motivates students, and identifies those ready for advanced stages.
3. Revision Status Report:
   * Content: List of students due for revision, overdue for revision, and those who have recently completed a revision. Can include the specific Surahs/Juz to be revised. Murajah work needs to be recycled every 5-10 days. Our current schedule is

4. Performance Trend Report:
   * Content: Graph/chart showing a student's memorization progress over time (e.g., Ayahs memorized per week).
   * Purpose: Visualizes progress, helps identify plateaus or improvements.
II. Group/Class Reports:
1. Class/Group Progress Overview:
   * Content: Summary of memorization progress for an entire class or group – average memorization per student, number of students at different stages (e.g., how many have completed 5 Juz, 10 Juz, etc.).
   * Purpose: Provides teachers/administrators with a high-level view of the group's performance.
2. Top Memorizers Report:
   * Content: List of students who have memorized the most new Ayahs/Surahs within a given timeframe.
   * Purpose: Encourages healthy competition and recognizes high achievers.
3. Struggling Students Report:
   * Content: Identifies students who are falling behind, have low memorization rates, or are consistently missing revisions.
   * Purpose: Allows teachers to provide targeted support and intervention.
III. Teacher/Admin Reports:
1. Teacher Workload Report:
   * Content: Number of students assigned to each teacher, total Ayahs/Surahs reviewed by each teacher, upcoming student assessments.
   * Purpose: Helps manage teacher assignments and assess workload distribution.
2. Attendance Report:
   * Content: Student attendance records for classes or one-on-one sessions.
   * Purpose: Tracks student engagement and consistency.
3. Assessment/Test Results Report:
   * Content: Scores or evaluations from student quarterly test before parent conferences.
   * Purpose: Measures comprehension and identifies areas where students struggle collectively.
4. Overall Program Statistics:
   * Content: Total number of students, total Ayahs/Surahs memorized across all students, average memorization time for the entire Quran (if applicable), retention rates.
   * Purpose: Provides a macroscopic view of the program's effectiveness and reach.
IV. Custom/Ad-Hoc Reports:
1. Filterable Student List:
   * Content: Allow filtering students by various criteria (e.g., gender, age group, current Juz, teacher, enrollment date).
   * Purpose: Provides flexibility for specific queries not covered by predefined reports.
2. Memorization Goals vs. Actual Progress:
   * Content: Compares each student's set memorization goals with their actual progress.
   * Purpose: Helps students and teachers track if goals are being met and adjust if necessary
